# Bundled menu-image provenance

`apps/ros/assets/menu/`. They were downloaded and converted to
480 × 360 WebP derivatives on **2026-07-16** for in-app menu-item selection.

They are sourced from Pexels under the [Pexels License](https://www.pexels.com/legal-pages/license/): use in a commercial app and modification are permitted, and attribution is not required. These files must remain embedded app assets rather than being offered as standalone stock-image downloads. They must not be used to imply a contributor's endorsement or as restaurant/trademark branding.

The SHA-256 values below identify the exact committed derivatives. `juice` and
`mocktail` intentionally use the same source and derivative, while representing
